About the "WONDER OF ASIYA HOTEL-Matara"


Right now, there is a real buzz about Sri Lanka, with those in the know making a beeline south of Matara for coastal spots. The lowdown on the most stunning beaches, the coolest hangouts and the best places to stay is at this hotel.. Cynics call it 'WONDER OF ASIYA' Well, yeah, you can see what they mean: Sri Lanka, with its polite, unruffled and uninvasive people, is an easy place to visit. Walk along any street in Matara, and drivers will definitely stop passing motor-rickshaw drivers to inquire about your transport requirements. But here's the funny thing: say no thank you, and they're going to give up and go down, really. "After them, you feel like shouting: "Come on, trouble me - I can handle it! It is also true that a place with the sheer cultural and spiritual overload of, say, Madurai or Varanasi is missing in Sri Lanka.

Offering beachfront accommodation 100 metres from Matara Beach, "Wonder of Asiya Hotel" is located in Matara and offers numerous facilities, including a garden and a private beach area. Room service is provided by this 3-star hotel. The accommodation includes a 24-hour front desk and a communal guest kitchen.This place is just great - it has very cozy bedrooms, a perfect swimming pool, close to a beach where you can finally see turtles swimming in the sea. If not, just wander around and grab something to eat and drink.
